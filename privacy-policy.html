Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data the bot collects and how it is used.

1. Data Collected

The bot may store the following information:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Survey responses submitted by users
  • Timestamps of interactions

2. How Data is Used

Data is used only for bot functionality, such as saving survey results and improving features.

3. Data Sharing

No data is sold or shared with third parties.

4. Storage

Data is stored securely and only kept as long as needed for bot functionality.

5. Contact

If you have questions, contact the bot developer via Discord.
